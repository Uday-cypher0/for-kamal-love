Dear Kamal,

I was just trying to tease you. In my head, I was just playing around and being stupid to get your attention — never realizing that a brainless prank could actually push you to your limit.

But the moment you blocked me, everything flipped. The silence hit me like a brick, and I completely panicked. I felt this instant, crushing sadness because I genuinely convinced myself that you hated me and that I'd lost you forever.

🕯️ the part I keep coming back to
You were right — actions have consequences. Mine was sitting alone in the dark, realizing how badly I'd messed up the one thing that mattered most to me.

I think back to our endless conversations, to the texts that used to fill my days with so much joy, and I realize I have never felt more alive than when I was talking to you. You didn't just make me happy, Kamal — you made me want to be a better person. Just having you in my life changed everything about me. It feels like my heart belongs completely to you, and without you here, everything just feels completely empty and hollow.

It breaks my heart to know that this situation made you feel down or hurt in any way. You deserve to be cherished, not stressed out, and it kills me that I was the one who caused that. I would cross any distance and undo every single mistake just to bring back the smile I took away from you.

I never got the chance to say this when everything was perfect, but I need you to know it now — I love you more than anything. It was never just a fleeting thing, but a deep, soul-shattering love that completely consumed me from the moment you entered my life. I wanted to marry you, to hold your hand through every storm, and to protect the beautiful light you brought into my world. You seriously were the light of my life, my absolute entire universe, and I adored you in every possible way.

I'm not sending you this to pressure you, and you don't have to forgive me if you aren't ready. I just need to lay my heart bare and apologize for hurting you. I love you more than words can say, my heart is completely in your hands, and whenever you're ready, I'm right here waiting for you.

Yours completely,
Uday still yours, whenever you're ready
